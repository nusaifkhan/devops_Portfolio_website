Re: DevOps Engineer Internship / Site Reliability Engineer Role
To: Hiring Manager

Dear Hiring Manager,

I am writing to express my strong interest in a DevOps Engineer Internship or Site Reliability Engineer position at your organization. As a BSc Software Engineering student at Cardiff Metropolitan University with hands-on experience in cloud automation, containerization, and CI/CD pipelines, I am eager to contribute to a team that treats infrastructure as code and deployment as a discipline.

My practical experience includes two real-world Azure projects that delivered measurable results. I automated VM lifecycle management using Python, Azure SDK, and Jenkins, reducing manual operations by 80% and cutting deployment time by 50%. I also built a cost-optimization tool that cleaned unused Azure resources, reducing cloud storage spend by 30%. These weren't just exercises — they were production-oriented solutions I designed, built, and validated end-to-end.

I hold 9+ certifications including Microsoft AZ-900 (Azure Fundamentals), FinOps Certified Practitioner, Kubernetes, Docker, Prometheus, Jenkins, Helm, and Linux — all earned within the past year. I'm currently pursuing AZ-104 and the Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) exam. My technical stack includes Terraform, Ansible, Grafana, and Bash scripting — tools I use daily.

What distinguishes me is my systems thinking. I don't just run commands; I understand why infrastructure behaves the way it does, how to design for failure, and how to reduce toil systematically. I'm the kind of engineer who reads postmortems for fun and automates repetitive tasks before being asked.

I would be thrilled to bring my skills, curiosity, and work ethic to your team. I'm available immediately for internship or junior roles and am happy to take on complex challenges. I would love the opportunity to discuss how I can contribute to your engineering goals.

Thank you sincerely for your time and consideration.
